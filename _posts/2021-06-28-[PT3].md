## 수렴

### $\textbf{Definition[simple]}$ 
A r.v. $X$ is **simple** if $X$ takes a finite number of values

<br/><br/>

### $\textbf{Theorem}$ 
If $X \geqslant 0, \exists $sequence of simple r.v.s $\lbrace X_n \rbrace$ s.t. $0 \leqslant X_n \leqslant X_{n+1} \nearrow X$
